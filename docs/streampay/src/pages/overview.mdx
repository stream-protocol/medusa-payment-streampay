Welcome to the Merchant Portal guide! As a merchant, having a centralized portal to manage, monitor, and analyze your transactions is crucial. The Merchant Portal is designed to offer you a seamless experience, ensuring that you have all the tools you need at your fingertips.

## Overview:

The Merchant Portal is a web-based interface that allows merchants to:

- **Monitor Transactions**: View all incoming and outgoing transactions in real-time.
- **Manage Payments**: Issue refunds, capture payments, and view payment details.
- **Analyze Data**: Access detailed analytics and insights to understand your sales trends.
- **Manage Settings**: Update your store details, payment preferences, and other settings.

## Getting Started:

### 1. **Logging In**:

To access the Merchant Portal:

1. Navigate to the Merchant Portal URL.
2. Enter your merchant credentials.
3. Click on the "Login" button.

### 2. **Dashboard Overview**:

Upon logging in, you'll be greeted with the dashboard. Here, you'll find:

- A summary of your sales and transactions.
- Recent transaction history.
- Quick action buttons for common tasks.

### 3. **Navigating the Portal**:

The portal is divided into several sections, accessible via the sidebar:

- **Transactions**: View a detailed list of all transactions.
- **Analytics**: Access charts and data visualizations of your sales trends.
- **Settings**: Manage your store and payment settings.
- **Support**: Get help or access the knowledge base.

## Key Features:

### 1. **Real-time Transaction Monitoring**:

Stay updated with real-time notifications for every transaction. Filter transactions based on date, amount, or status.

### 2. **Detailed Analytics**:

Understand your customers better with detailed analytics. Track sales trends, popular products, and customer demographics.

### 3. **Easy Refunds**:

Issue refunds directly from the portal. Select the transaction, specify the refund amount, and process it with a single click.

### 4. **Secure Access**:

The portal uses advanced encryption and security measures to ensure that your data is safe. Enable two-factor authentication for added security.
